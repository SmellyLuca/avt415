User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Ramsey is a social media influencer, and posts photos of his outfits daily. He's starting to run out of outfits and doesn't want to repeat the same outfit, so he looks online for some new footwear that'll have his followers engaging with his posts more. He finds Monte Bronco, and browses around the site and social media to see how they style their prodcuts. He finds a few he likes, and places an order online.

Actor

Ramsey

Motivator

Post outfit pictures for social media

Intention

Ramsey wants to show off new footwear most people don't know about to have people engage with his post.

Action

Ramsey browses the site, and looks at the brand's social media to see how they style their products.

Resolution

Ramsey finds a few options he likes, and places an order online.
